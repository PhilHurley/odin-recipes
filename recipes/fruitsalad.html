Fruit Salad

Description

Can be made with almost any fruit.

Remember Inteligence is knowing a tomato is a fruit, wisdom is knowing it doesn't belong in fruit salad.

Ingredients

  • Whatever fruit you like
  • Some orange juice, or other juice if you prefer

Instructions

  1. If necessary chop the fruit to appropriate size pieces
  2. Put the prepared fruit into a bowl
  3. Pour the fruit juice over the fruit
  4. Leave in a fridge to soak until you are ready to eat it

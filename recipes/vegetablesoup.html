Vegetable Soup

Description

A soup made with vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Whatever vegetables you have available/want to use

Instructions

  1. Gently fry sliced onion, until starting to go translucent.
  2. Add the other vegetables and saute for about five minutes.
  3. Cover the ingredients with water or stock, bring to the boil and reduce to a simmer until vegetables soften.
  4. Once cooked you can either blend the soup or leave it as a chunky 'rural,' texture.
  5. Serve with crusty bread and butter.

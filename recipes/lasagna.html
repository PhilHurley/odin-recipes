Lasagna

Description

An Italian classic

A rich beef ragu sandwiched between layers of pasta and smothered in a creamy, cheesy sauce.

Serves well with a nice green salad, garlic bread if you love those carbs!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tin of tomatoes
  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Fresh herbs
  1. Fry up the onion and garlic in a little oil for about a minute.
  2. Add the meat and mix together, fry over a medium heat until the meat starts to brown.
  3. Season the frying mixture with salt and pepper, then add the tomatoes and reduce the heat.
  4. Whilst that bubbles away prepare a normal white sauce and add the cheese to it (reserving some for topping
